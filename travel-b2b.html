Who we are

We operate Japan
from the inside.

Aconcagua K.K. is a luxury ground operator based in Kyoto. We work with hotels, golf courses, transport providers, venues and international agencies that need a reliable, high-level partner on the ground in Japan.

We don't sell travel packages. We provide the operational backbone that makes exceptional experiences possible — with the discretion, precision and local knowledge your clients deserve.

What we do

Ground services,
at every level.

Local guides with deep cultural knowledge and high language proficiency. Available across Kyoto, Tokyo, Osaka, Nara, Hiroshima, Hakone and beyond — for individual travellers, groups and corporate delegations.

End-to-end coordination on the ground: transfers, in-destination reservations, on-trip accompaniment and real-time problem solving. A single point of contact managing every detail, so nothing is left to chance.

Professional interpreters for business meetings, institutional visits, negotiations and corporate travel. Japanese, English and Spanish. Delivered with the discretion and precision that high-level engagements demand.

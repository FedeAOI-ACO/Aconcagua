Our Founders

Co-Founder

Federico Drasal

Chief Executive Officer

A passionate dreamer from a young age, Federico always sought knowledge and experiences of Asian cultures. His childhood interest evolved into a desire to create stable bridges that foster mutual understanding among distant cultures.

With a background in cinema, philosophy and extensive studies in Indian, Chinese and Japanese culture, he embarked on a quest to explore the foreign. After over 15 years of working all around the globe with NGOs to promote Asian economy and culture, in 2019 he started his project in Japan.

Based in

Kyoto & Europe

Languages

ES · EN · JP · IT · PT · TIB

Co-Founder

Agustín Pérez

Chief Operating Officer

A former mathematics and architecture scholar turned educator in Argentina, Agustín embarked on a journey of cultural exploration after meeting his Japanese wife in Latin America. A decade-long devotion to learning Japanese and immersing in the country's rich heritage led to the establishment of a company in Kyoto.

Specialising in trading, consulting, multilingual services, and cultural propagation, the venture reflects Agustín's passion for cross-cultural exchange and language education.

Based in

Kyoto, Japan

Languages

ES · EN · JP

Background

Mathematics & Architecture
